How It Works
From order to advertised routes.
STEP 01
Provision the Port
We turn up a 1G, 10G or 100G port at your chosen PoP or over a Last Mile tail, with diverse options available.
STEP 02
Establish BGP
We configure an eBGP session to your ASN, apply prefix filters, and validate against IRR and RPKI ROAs.
STEP 03
Advertise & Optimise
Your prefixes go live with full BGP community control, letting you steer traffic and tune the route mix.

The detail that matters.

Routing Security
RPKI ROA validation, IRR-based prefix filtering, and max-prefix limits applied on every session by default.
Traffic Control
Granular BGP communities for geographic and per-peer steering, plus blackhole community for DDoS mitigation.
