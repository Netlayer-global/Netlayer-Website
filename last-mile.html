What is Last Mile?
Bridge to the backbone.

Last Mile connects your premises — wherever they are — into our metro, international transport and backbone services. By integrating diverse local providers across 30+ markets, we extend our reach to your edge locations and remote offices, all delivered and managed under a single Netlayer contract.
