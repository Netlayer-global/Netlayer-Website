What is DC Wave?
Data centre to data centre.

DC Wave delivers dedicated, high-capacity DWDM wavelengths between data centres across the metro region. Ideal for storage replication, backup, and high-throughput inter-DC traffic, it provides predictable, low-latency performance with the protection and diversity your workloads demand.

Technical Specifications
The detail that matters.
Technology
DWDM (Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing) over metro fibre rings.
Capacity
Dedicated wavelengths from 10G to 400G per lambda, with multiple lambdas per fibre pair.
Client Interfaces
10GE, 100GE and 400GE handoffs, plus OTU and Fibre Channel for storage extension.
Protection
Optical protection switching with diverse fibre paths for sub-50ms failover.
Latency
Deterministic, ultra-low metro latency — ideal for synchronous replication.
SLA
99.99% availability with protected routing and 24/7 NOC monitoring.
