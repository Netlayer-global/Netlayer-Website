What is EPL?
Flexible metro Ethernet.

Ethernet Private Line delivers dedicated, point-to-point Ethernet connectivity across the metro region. Perfect for businesses entering a new market, EPL lets you scale bandwidth up and down effortlessly — with the flexibility to adapt contract terms, backhaul routes and providers as your needs evolve.
