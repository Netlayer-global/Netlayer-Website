What is IX Peering?
Closer to the content.

Remote IX peering lets you access major Internet Exchanges without a physical presence at each one. Connect once to the Netlayer backbone and reach exchanges across APAC and beyond — lowering latency and improving end-user experience while keeping full control of your peering strategy.

Why choose Netlayer
  • Access the most popular IXs without local hardware
  • Reduce transit costs by peering directly with networks
  • Lower latency to content and eyeball networks
  • Backed by deep APAC exchange partnerships
  • Single connection, multiple exchange reach
